TRAINING NOTES

TRAINING NOTES is a tool that will help you practice your reading and your scales and chords on a guitar, piano or any instrument.

How to use
How to use:

You can choose the group of notes you want to train.

5th

You can change the order in which the notes will be displayed: regular, random or by fifths.

You can move through the scale manually.

You can move through the scale automatically, with the "auto-change" function.

You can customize the "auto-change" interval or pick one from list on the side.

N

You can choose the way the notes are displayed.

You can choose which notes will be displayed on the pentagram: bottom half, top half or the whole pentagram.
