About Me

My name is Fabián. I was born and raised in Montevideo, Uruguay, and have been living in Madrid for more than fifteen years. In 2018 I decided to turn my professional life around and dive into the world of Web Development (Frontend and Backend).

Neko Verde ("cat" in japanese, "green" in spanish) is a proyect driven by the need to carry out my ideas and give shape to my passions, merge creativity, style and functionality, drawing all of it with code.
